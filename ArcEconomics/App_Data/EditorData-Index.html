ARCeconomics was established in 2005 to assume the U.S.-based work of GHK International (www.ghkint.com).  In 2012 we moved to Canada and continue to provide evaluation and economic services to governments, foundations and private sector clients.  Our goal is to achieve the highest levels of use of the knowledge, advice and insights provided by ARCeconomics to improve the programs and services of our clients.  We work primarily in North America, Africa and Asia as well as Europe, and the Pacific.   ARCeconomics is led by Andy Rowe who is recognized as a leader in evaluation methods for environmental and resource settings.
ARCeconomics is located on Salt Spring Island, British Columbia, Canada.
